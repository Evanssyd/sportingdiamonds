In my experience, this is what it takes to win at the college level. I teach a pitching style that is easy to learn and allows a young pitcher to grow quickly and use more kinds of breaking pitches while throwing hard to spots. I aim to create strong, self-confident pitchers, who are competitive and more successful than the other pitchers around them. I want my pitchers to work hard, be strong on the field, and have fun. I trained for years with the best pitching coaches in the country and know that the inner pitcher, sportsmanship, courage, pride, and laughter are key tools on the mound. I look forward to developing a new pitcher as we have fun and strive to make her the best. Make her stand out
